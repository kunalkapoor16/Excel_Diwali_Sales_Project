# Excel_Diwali_Sales_Project
This project focuses on analyzing Diwali sales data to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and key business insights. The dataset contains transactional information such as customer demographics, product categories, order details, and revenue generated during the festive season. The goal of this analysis is to help businesses understand their customers better and optimize marketing strategies to increase sales during high-demand periods like Diwali.

**Objectives:** 
Analyze customer demographics and buying behavior, 
identify top-performing product categories, 
determine high-revenue generating states and zones, 
understand purchasing patterns based on age, gender, and occupation, 
provide actionable insights for business growth.

**Key Insights:**
Identification of top spending customer segments, 
analysis of gender-wise and age-wise purchasing trends, 
determination of highest revenue-generating states and zones, 
recognition of most popular product categories, 
insights into customer occupation vs spending behavior.

**Results & Conclusion:** 
The analysis highlights key customer segments contributing the most to sales, helping businesses target the right audience.
It also reveals which product categories and regions generate maximum revenue, enabling better inventory and marketing decisions during festive seasons.
